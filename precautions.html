COVID-19 INDIA UPDATES

#IndiaFightsCovid-19

Do's

Wash Your Hands

Regular hand wash for 30 seconds will help you avoid germs or infection. Use soap or hand sanitizer for better results and do this every time you travel or touch anything, especially after going to the bathroom, before eating, and after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing.

Cover Your Mouth & Nose

Covering your mouth and nose while sneezing or when anyone next to coughs or sneezes can do you a lot better. Coronavirus usually spreads through cough and covering your nose and mouth will save you from this epidemic.

Stay At Home

Try being at home until it's not very necessary. Avoid being in crowded places. An infected person can spread the virus instantly and crowded places is a good way to accomplish this. Make sure you wear full sleeves shirts with anti-pollution masks when you travel. Staying indoors is a rather safe option.

Maintain Social Distance

Social distancing remains a key way to mitigate spread.Avoid close contact with people who are sick. If possible, maintain 6 feet (about 2 arms’ length) between the person who is sick and other household members and with people who don’t live in your household. Keeping distance from others is especially important for people who are at higher risk of getting very sick.

Consult A Doctor If Sick

If suffering from a common cold, cough, nausea, vomiting, shortness of breath and fatigue make it a point to consult a doctor at the earliest. Any of these symptoms could be a sign that you are suffering from the virus. Consult a doctor rather than opt for self-medication that can leave you battling with a deadly illness. Say no to antibiotics as it would later lead to antibiotic resistance.

Dont's

Avoid Close Contact With Anyone

Do not get close to anyone, especially touching or laughing closely. Also, use anti-pollution masks when out with friends or family. Avoid touching anyone and do not use the same utensils used by another. These simple non-touchy ways can do good until the outbreak comes to an end.

Do Not Spit

Spitting can increase the spread of the virus. Avoid spiting at in public and home. Also, avoid getting close to a sick person suffering from cold and cough. Spitting is a criminal offence in the present time.

Avoid Using Public Transport

Travelling by cab, flight, bus, train and tramp can get you infected. Use anti-pollution masks and carry a hand sanitizer with you everywhere. Consider travelling by your own vehicle and avoid public transport. In case you are feeling sick, it is advised that you avoid travelling and consult a doctor at the earliest.

Avoid Public Gathering

Avoid public gatherings as much as possible. Step out of your house only if necessary. If going at a crowded place, cover your face with a face mask and also a face shield if needed. Use sanitizer after touching anything. Wear thin gloves. Always have a bath after returning home. These measures can avoid the transmission of virus to a greater extent.

Don’t Touch Your Face
Do not touch your face, nose and mouth often. This avoids the risks of developing the virus. Don't touch your mask directly from the area around mouth and nose. Always remove or adjust the mask with the help of the ear support string only. Wash your hands with soap or hand sanitizer and this will do you good.
